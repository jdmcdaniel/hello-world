Days For Girls , the workings and sewing DfG Kits

Days for Girls is a womens outreach non-profit volunteer organization. An outreach for girls education in other countries and are deprived of having a feminine hygiene care. This depravation cause girls to stay at away from school during their monthly Period. And thus, they fall behind in school and the dropout

Volunteer women around teh world have reached out to help alivatied this situation by providing Feminine Hygiene products. Chapters and Teams consist of volunteers who gather together sew and assemble the DfG Kits.

DfG-RB supplies, kits, education, and water wells

In 2018, Days For Girls, Rancho Bernardo collaborated with other missionary works which also then drilled water wells at the schools site, so fresh, clean water was available at the schools as most locations are rural villages not cities or municipalities. Thus, local schools and home life no longer needs spend hours fetching water from the distant stream.

The Rancho Bernardo Day for Girls, working in Ghana

Days For Girls International goes worldwide and has many outreaches into many deprived countries. The Rancho Bernardo chapter (in San Diego North County) works with the country of Ghana. This pairing was done as a collaborative effort with some local Ghana Days For Girls Enterprises working in Ghana. Currently, there are some efforts or materials which can’t be provided directly by these enterprises, and the Rancho Bernardo DfG is supplementing them. Primarily special fabrics, shield materials, and panties. See image below for the “shields” pads.

 

 

In terms of Feminine Hygiene Education, Days For Girls have instituted its own training program and ed materials for locals to pitch and field questions from the school girls. The collaboration of using Ghana locals via their enterprises aids the program’s success and acceptance. Because of local customs and norms, the Feminine Hygiene Pad is the solution of choice, and a washable version reduces other sanitary issues with raw disposal (as disposal pads end up on the side of the roads, prone for spreading disease with the dogs in such grabbing the materials). The pad is fitted into a special pair of panties that hold the pads, that can also then be a washed without a showing/violating cultural taboos.
